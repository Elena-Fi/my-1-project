The most popular movie genres for watching according to a survey among viewers

Home Take part in the survey

About survey Two hundred and fifty participances between the ages of 18 and 55 were interviewed and asked which of genre of movie they prefer watching. And there were 50 percent of female and 50 percent of male. In general among of top genres were action & adventure, comedy, drama and romance. So, 55 percent of viewers prefer watching action & adventure, 55 percent prefer watching comedy, 45 percent prefer watching drama, and finally 45 percent of participances prefer watching romance.
