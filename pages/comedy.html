Comedy movies

Comedy movies are a film genre designed to amuse, entertain, and provoke laughter through humor, witty dialogue, exaggeration, and absurd situations. They often feature lighthearted plots, happy endings, and aim to provide escapism, with subgenres ranging from romantic and slapstick to satirical and dark comedy.
    Common Comedy Subgenres:
  • Romantic Comedy (Rom-Com): Focuses on a humorous, lighthearted love story.
  • Slapstick/Farce: Emphasizes exaggerated physical activity and ridiculous situations.
  • Satire/Parody: Mocks other films, genres, or societal norms.
  • Dark Comedy: Finds humor in taboo, serious, or painful subjects.
